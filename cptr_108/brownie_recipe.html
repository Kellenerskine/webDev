Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Optional - 1.2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Melt butter on stovetop
  2. Add cocoa
  3. Add sugar
  4. This should make a thick dark paste.
  5. Quickly stir in eggs, vanilla and then flour.
  6. Grease a baking sheet
  7. Bake on 325 for 15-20 minutes.
